About Us

Botswana Information Technology Society (BITS), a non-profit professional organization that represents various IT stakeholders in Botswana. The mission of BITS is to promote the understanding and adoption of IT, encourage an effective IT industry, and develop effective people.


Responsibilities of a society's council, including mobilizing resources, managing assets, and convening meetings.



The Botswana Information Technology Society (BITS) is more than just a non-profit professional organization - it's a movement fueled by passion and a commitment to the advancement of the IT industry in Botswana. With a mission to promote the understanding and adoption of IT, BITS strives to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed in the digital age.

As a council, BITS takes on the important responsibility of mobilizing resources, managing assets, and convening meetings that bring together like-minded individuals and organizations to share ideas, build partnerships, and drive progress. Join BITS today and become part of a community that's dedicated to making a real difference in the world of IT!
