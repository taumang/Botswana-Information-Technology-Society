Membership

the benefits of being a member of the Botswana Information Technology Society (BITS), which includes assistance in meeting IT standards, keeping up-to-date with IT developments, access to BITS publications, participation in professional activities, job marketability, interaction with IT professionals, international connections, government recognition, professional advice, professional affiliation, and interaction with peers through specialist interest groups and user interest groups.

Eligible Members

The Society accepts membership from: Graduates with IT-related qualifications that conform to the Society's education standards IT professionals Students pursuing IT-related qualifications that conform to the Society's education standards Corporate entities and institutions interested in IT Educational institutions that offer IT-related qualifications conforming to the Society's education standards Professional bodies with reciprocal arrangements Individuals interested in IT who are not qualified to practice under the Society's constitution.
