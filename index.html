Promoting The IT Profession

BITS is a non-profit organization in Botswana that represents diverse IT stakeholders with a common interest in driving innovation and maintaining high standards of practice in the IT profession. They strive to maximize returns on IT investments in the country.

TICON Africa Conference

Experience the revolutionary Inaugural TICONA conference, propelling Africa into the future with cutting-edge innovations. Hosted by ICTAU in vibrant Kampala, this event unites ICT talent from across the continent, creating a brighter future. TICONA challenges technology trends, empowers professionals, and offers an engaging experience. Join us on the journey to power Africa beyond 2030, setting the stage for unprecedented ICT advancements.

Specialist and User Interest Groups

BITS encourages members to form SIG and UIG based on shared IT interests for exchange of ideas.

User Interest Groups

User BITS groups are interest groups formed by members with shared interest in specific IT systems or application areas.

Specialist Groups

Specialist BITS groups are interest groups formed by members who share a common interest in specialized areas of IT, such as programming, security, and project management.
